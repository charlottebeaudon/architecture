OAXACA SANATORIUM
Studio Fortbau_Ecology Sustainability Cultural Heritage_SS21

Oaxaca Sanatorium in southern Mexico is a facility that provides aid and shelter to local people who have suffered traumata from state inflicted violence and discrimination. The project is a fortbau of a bamboo roof structure built by Angewante students on the same location in 2004, as part of a permaculture research facility. The fortbau was developed by analysing the single structural elements of the case study - double layers of roof, columns, spacers, concret cross reinforcements, earth bricks - and scaling them and thus shifting their functionality and providing them with a new program.
