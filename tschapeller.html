Wreck for two is a poetic analysis based on stimulations extracted from the area of the OMV Zentraltanklager at the Danube around the year 2100. It is a report from the future in a strange foreign, yet familiar language which seems to be both: full of meaning and emptied of all meanings simultaneously.
