Battery City Athens_GLC_SS23

After the tabula rasa in Athens imagines a new spatial configuration of the Koumoundourou square after it has been erased as the title suggests. A parabolic mega structure casts soft shadows onto the ground and provides cooling. Since the panels are lifted in the air, people can navigate the ground without obstruction.The adjacent IKA building, an empty ruin becomes accessible to the public by adding pavement and pillars on the P.Tsaldari street and removing the first slab of the IKA.
